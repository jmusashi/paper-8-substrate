\## Canonical Description



The following figure presents the structural topology of the 3Sync architecture.

Three axes — Stigmergy, HiveSync, and Decision Continuity Engineering (DCE) —

converge on the central 3Sync coordination layer, which interfaces with the shared

environment and the EOI identity boundary of each agent.



Stigmergy provides indirect environmental signals to the central 3Sync coordination

layer, which integrates HiveSync (invariant alignment), DCE (decision continuity),

EOI (identity boundary), and the shared environment through tri-axis coherence.

A feedback loop illustrates how 3Sync deposits signals back into the environment,

completing the stigmergic cycle.

\## Notes for Visual Reproduction



When reproducing Figure 1 as a formal diagram:

\- The three axes should be depicted as arrows converging on the central 3Sync layer

\- The EOI boundary should encapsulate each individual agent

\- The feedback loop from 3Sync back to the environment must be shown

\- The shared environment sits outside individual agent EOI boundaries

\- No direct agent-to-agent communication arrows should be present
